📖 User Guide
Portugal Golf 2026
The home screen gives you a quick overview of the whole trip. At the top you'll see the number of players, days, Tour Championship pot and the current leader.

Tour Championship — Cumulative Stableford points across Days 1, 2 & 4. €10 entry per player (7 players = €70 pot). 🥇 €40 · 🥈 €20 · 🥉 €10.

Daily Prizes Breakdown — Shows prize money available each day. Days 1 & 2 have a €40 pot, Day 3 (Scramble) €35, Day 4 (Individual) €25.

The Courses — Flyover videos and links to scorecards, slope ratings and course info for both the Faldo and O'Connor Jnr. courses.
Player Roster — Each player has a name, handicap index and confidence rating. Tap a player's avatar to open their profile and add a photo, bio and custom ratings.

Confidence ratings — 🟢 Plays to handicap (−1 effective), 🟡 About right (no change), 🔴 Might struggle (+1 effective). Used for team balancing only.

Re-draw Teams — Splits 8 players into two balanced teams of 4 based on effective handicap. Hit it multiple times to cycle through different balanced combinations. The balance tolerance (shots) controls how many options are available — higher = more variety, slightly less balance.

Generate Pairings — Creates pairings for all 4 days automatically. The schedule is optimised so every player meets every opponent across the trip, with no one facing the same person more than twice.

Swap players — Use the Swap dropdown on any player in the team cards to manually swap them with someone from the other team.
Each day tab shows the format, tee picker, pairings, results and an AI summary.

Tee picker — Select the tee colour for the day. The slope and course rating update automatically and feed into the handicap calculations.

📐 Official Format Handicaps — Calculates each player's correct handicap allowance for the day's format (85% for 4BBB, 35%/15% for Scramble, 100% for Individual) using slope and course rating. Tap Apply to use these in scoring. Tap ↺ Reset to Normal to go back to standard handicaps.

Pairings card — Shows the groups for the day. Admins can reveal or hide pairings for each day — non-admins see a placeholder until revealed. Day 1 is always visible.

Results & Prize Winners — Enter the winning pair/player for each prize category, nearest pin winners and match results for team scoring. Everything saves automatically.

🤣 Day Summary / 🎙 Pre-Tour Preview — Hit Generate to get an AI-written humorous summary. Before Day 1 scores are in it writes a savage pre-tour player preview. Needs a Cloudflare Worker configured (tap ⚙).
Money Leaderboard — Shows how much each player has won across all days. Updates automatically as results are entered. The totals row shows won / available so you can see which days still need results entered.

Tour Championship — Live standings based on cumulative Stableford scores across Days 1, 2 & 4. 🥇 €40 · 🥈 €20 · 🥉 €10.

Daily Results Summary — All prize categories for all 4 days. Shows TBD until a winner has been entered on the relevant day tab.
Tour Championship Standings — Ranked by total Stableford points across Days 1, 2 & 4. Each day's score shown individually.

Team Scores — Running team totals across all 4 days. Days 1–3 award points for match results; Day 4 awards points for individual 1v1 match wins.
Photos are organised by day (Days 1–6). Select a day, then tap 📷 Add Photo to upload from your device. Enter your name so everyone knows who took it.

Tap any photo to view it full size. You can add comments on any photo — they sync to all devices in real time.

Photos are stored in the cloud (Cloudinary) so they're available to everyone regardless of device.
🔒 Lock button (top-right of nav) — Tap to lock or unlock editing. Unlock requires the admin password. The lock is per-device — each device must unlock independently.

What's locked — Scores, results, teams, pairings, players, team names, tees, handicap overrides, AI settings and pairings reveal state all require admin to change.

What's free — Player profiles, ratings, photos, logistics and comments can be edited by anyone.

👁 Reveal Pairings — On each day tab, admins can reveal or hide that day's pairings. Non-admins see a placeholder until revealed. Day 1 is always visible.

🔓 Unlock Redraw for All — On the Players tab, admins can temporarily unlock team redrawing and balance tolerance for everyone — useful for letting the group play around before the trip. Lock it again once you go live.
Everything syncs automatically across all devices every 15 seconds via Firebase. The sync dot in the top bar shows the status — green means in sync, grey means offline or waiting.

Data is saved locally on your device immediately when you make a change, then pushed to the cloud. When you open the app or switch tabs it pulls the latest from the cloud.

No internet? — The app works offline. Changes save locally and sync automatically once you're back online.
